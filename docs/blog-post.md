Just launched the **OJT Requirements Tracker** — a free web app that helps students track all their Pre-OJT and Post-OJT requirements in one place. No login, no app to download, no email required. Just a student ID and you're in.

## Why I Built This

Every semester, I see students struggling to keep track of their OJT requirements. They ask:

- "Ma'am, ano na po kulang ko?"
- "Nasubmit ko na ba 'yung Parent Consent?"
- "Na-verify na po ba 'yung Medical ko?"

This tracker solves all of that. Students can check their own progress anytime, and coordinators can see everything in real-time.

## How It Works

**For Students:**

1. **Register** — Enter your SR-Code (format: `01-00001`), then fill in your name, section, semester, program, and optional company.
2. **Pre-OJT Checklist** — 8 requirements to complete before starting OJT: Parent Consent, Personal History Statement, Acceptance Form, Internship Agreement, Endorsement Letter, Registration Form, Medical Clearance, and School ID. Check off each one as you submit it.
3. **Post-OJT Checklist** — Unlocks automatically once all Pre-OJT requirements are done. 6 requirements including Performance Appraisal, Supervisor Feedback, Technical Narrative Report, and more.
4. **Look Up Anytime** — Just type your SR-Code and see your full record. Update your company if your assignment changes.

**For Coordinators:**

A separate dashboard lets coordinators view all students, their progress, and verify submitted requirements. Everything is in one Google Sheet, accessible anytime.
