## SCENE 3 — Step 1: Registration [2:00–4:00]

**Visual**: Type URL → cursor to SR-Code input → fill form → click Register

**Narration**:
> "Let's start with **Registration**. If this is your first time using the tracker, here's exactly what to do."
>
> "Step one: Enter your **SR-Code**. The format is simple — two digits, a hyphen, and five digits. Like this: `01-00001`. You'll find this on your school ID."
>
> "Hit **Enter** or click the **Register** button."
>
> *(registration success toast appears)*
>
> "Once you're registered, a section called **Update Profile** will automatically appear. This is where you enter your details — your full name, section, semester, program, and the company where you'll be doing your OJT."
>
> "The **Company** field is optional. You can fill it in later or update it anytime — perfect if your assignment changes mid-semester."
>
> "Once everything's filled in, click **Update Profile**. Your information is now saved in the system."

**On-screen text**:
```
SR-Code:    01-00001 ✓
Name:       Juan Dela Cruz
Section:    BSIT-4A
Semester:   1st
Program:    BS Information Technology
Company:    [optional]
```

## SCENE 4 — Step 2: Pre-OJT Checklist [4:00–6:30]

**Visual**: Scroll to Pre-OJT checklist, cursor hovers over each checkbox

**Narration**:
> "Now here's the most important part — the **Pre-OJT Requirements Checklist**."
>
> "These are the eight documents you need to submit **before** you start your OJT:"
>
> "Parent Consent, Personal History Statement, Acceptance Form, Internship Agreement, Endorsement Letter, Registration Form, Medical Clearance, and School ID."
>
> "Each requirement has a checkbox next to it. When you submit a document — just click the box."
>
> *(cursor clicks checkbox — checkmark appears)*
>
> "Your checks are saved **locally** in your browser, so they won't disappear if you refresh the page. But to make sure your coordinator sees your progress — you need to click the **Save Pre-OJT Progress** button."
>
> *(cursor clicks Save — toast appears)*
>
> "Once you do, you'll see a confirmation message at the top of the screen: 'Pre-OJT progress saved!' That means your coordinator can now see your updated status."

**On-screen text**: 💡 **Tip**: Check off what you've submitted → Click Save → Coordinator sees it instantly.
